Record 5a - Temperature Control Log - Cook/Cool/Reheat & Serve

Date Produced / Batch No Item Description Process Start Time Finish Time Temp °C Time 1 Temp °C (60°) Time 2 Temp °C (21°) Time 3 Temp °C (5°) Serve Time Serve Temp °C Initial
Processes:

Cook all potentially hazardous Food To at least 75°C
Reheat cooled potentially hazardous food that is to be served hot to at least 60°C

Cooked Potentially hazardous food that is being cooled for later use must be cooled from:
60°C to 21°C within the first 2 hours. It must then be cooled from
21°C to below 5°C within the next 4 hours (6 hours in all)
